Pierogi - Polish Dumplings

Traditional Polish recipe

This recipe is polish favorite dish passed on from generation to generation. Traditionally make for Christmas, but can be made for any ocasion with many different filligs.

Ingredients

Sauerkraut Filling:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/3 cup chopped onion
  • 1 1/2 cups sauerkrauf, drained and minced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Potato Filling:

  • 3 table spoons butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cups cold mashed potatoes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper

Dough:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
